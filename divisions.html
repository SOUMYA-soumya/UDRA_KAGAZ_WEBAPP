Two Divisions. One Mission.

How we segregate waste to maximize social and commercial impact.

📘

PUNARJEEVAN

The Reuse Division

Punarjeevan is the heart of our educational mission. We collect discarded practical files directly from university lab stores. Our SHG workers manually extract the blank, unused pages from these files. These pages are guillotine-trimmed to a factory-clean edge and spiral-bound into 100-page premium notebooks.


These notebooks are donated entirely to underprivileged children, carrying the co-branding of the host University and the CSR sponsor.

  • 6,000 notebooks generated per campus/year
  • Funded by targeted CSR Grants
  • Provides University NAAC Criterion 7 documentation
📜

NIRMAAN

The Recycle Division

Nirmaan is the commercial engine that sustains our operations. After Punarjeevan extracts the blank pages, we are left with the written/used pages and cardboard covers. Instead of trashing these, we ship them to a central plant.


This waste is pulped and transformed into premium handmade paper products—diaries, card stock, and notepads—providing steady micro-employment for SHG women while generating self-sustaining commercial revenue.

  • Premium diaries & notepads
  • Self-sustaining commercial revenue
  • SHG Women Empowerment at the grassroots level
