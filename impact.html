We provide detailed quarterly and annual impact reports containing exact data: weight of paper diverted from landfills, notebooks donated in the university's name, and the equivalent carbon offset. These metrics directly plug into NAAC Criterion 7.

Corporate sponsors (like MCL or Hindalco) fund the logistical aspects of the Punarjeevan division (transport, binding machinery, SHG wages). In return, the donated notebooks carry the CSR sponsor's branding alongside the University's logo, fulfilling their grassroots education mandate.
