From Idea to Movement

The UKI Journey

Every revolution starts with a single act of defiance against the status quo.

The Spark

The Observation

Walking past the VSSUT lab store, Soumyaranjan noticed mountains of discarded practical record files — perfectly good blank pages destined for scrap dealers at ₹5/kg. A thought struck: "What if we intercepted this before it became waste?"

The Insight

Two Streams, One Source

The key breakthrough: each discarded file contains both blank AND written pages. By segregating them, we create TWO value streams from ONE waste source — reuse (notebooks) and recycling (handmade paper). Nobody else was doing this.

Year 1 — Pilot

VSSUT Burla Launch

First MoU signed with VSSUT. PUNARJEEVAN goes live — 6,000 spiral-bound notebooks created and donated to underprivileged children in Sambalpur district. CSR pitch campaigns begin with MCL and Hindalco.

📘 6,000 notebooks 🏫 1 campus
Year 2 — Grow

NIT Rourkela + ITER Join

3 campuses operational. NIRMAAN plant becomes profitable. Alumni sales channels go live with premium handcrafted diaries and notepads. SHG women network expands across districts.

📘 18,000 notebooks 🏫 3 campuses 👩‍🏭 24 SHG jobs
Year 3 — Scale

Odisha-Wide Operations

8 campuses across Odisha. Second NIRMAAN recycling plant commissioned. Online e-commerce store launched for the premium Nirmaan Collection. 48,000 notebooks donated to government schools.

📘 48,000 notebooks 🏫 8 campuses 🌳 1,920 trees saved
Year 4-5 — The Vision

Pan-India Movement

25 campuses across Odisha, Jharkhand, and Chhattisgarh. ₹2.08 Cr annual revenue. 156 SHG jobs created. 1,50,000 notebooks donated. India's first organized academic paper upcycling network.

📘 1,50,000 notebooks 🏫 25 campuses 👩‍🏭 156 SHG jobs 💰 ₹2.08 Cr revenue
