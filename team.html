Meet the Founder

Every movement begins with one person who refuses to look away.

SS
✉️ 💼

Soumyaranjan Sahoo

Founder & CEO VSSUT Burla

"The 6,00,000 pages sitting in VSSUT's bins are not waste. They are the raw material for a movement."

As an engineering student at VSSUT Burla, Soumyaranjan witnessed firsthand the massive mountains of discarded practical record files — each containing dozens of perfectly blank, unused pages — being sold to scrap dealers for just ₹5-₹10 per kilogram.

While others walked past this daily, he saw an opportunity to solve three problems at once: educational access for underprivileged children, sustainable livelihoods for SHG (Self-Help Group) women, and environmental conservation through circular waste management.

What started as a simple observation has grown into Udra Kagaz Innovations — a dual-division social enterprise with a clear mission: intercept academic waste before the scrap dealer, and transform it into notebooks for children and premium handcrafted paper products for commerce.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide every decision we make.

Sustainability First

Every decision is measured against its environmental footprint. We prove that doing good and doing business are not mutually exclusive.
